## Design A: RG316 on Single FT-240-31

| Item        | Qty           | Notes                                         |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------------------------------------|
| FT-240-31   | 1             | Amidon / Fair-Rite mix 31 toroid              |
| RG316 coax  | ~100 cm (40") | 12 turns (~60 mm/turn) + 10 cm tail each side |

*Per-turn path on a single FT-240 with RG316 ≈ 60 mm. 12 turns ≈ 720 mm
plus ~10 cm of tail at each end for the SO-239 solder joints → ~90–100 cm.
Buy 1.2 m (4 ft) for comfort.*
